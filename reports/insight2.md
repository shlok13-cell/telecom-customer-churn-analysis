# Key Business Insights

## Customer Churn Analysis

### Insight 1: Younger Customers Show Higher Churn Tendency

Analysis indicates that younger customer segments are more likely to churn compared to older customers.

Business Impact:

* Reduced customer lifetime value
* Increased acquisition costs

Recommendation:

* Launch targeted retention campaigns for younger customers
* Introduce personalized offers and loyalty programs

---

### Insight 2: Higher Monthly Charges Are Associated With Churn

Customers paying higher monthly charges demonstrate a greater tendency to leave the service.

Business Impact:

* Revenue leakage from premium customers

Recommendation:

* Review pricing strategies
* Introduce bundled plans and loyalty discounts

---

### Insight 3: Active Members Retain Better

Customers actively engaging with services show lower churn rates.

Business Impact:

* Higher retention and recurring revenue

Recommendation:

* Increase customer engagement programs
* Promote value-added services

---

### Insight 4: Multi-Product Customers Demonstrate Stronger Retention

Customers using multiple telecom products tend to remain with the company longer.

Business Impact:

* Increased customer lifetime value

Recommendation:

* Promote cross-selling opportunities
* Offer bundled telecom packages

---

### Insight 5: Network Quality Has Limited Influence

Network quality shows only a weak relationship with churn compared to pricing and engagement factors.

Business Impact:

* Network improvements alone may not significantly reduce churn

Recommendation:

* Focus retention efforts on customer engagement and pricing optimization

---

# Strategic Recommendations

1. Launch targeted youth retention campaigns
2. Introduce customer loyalty reward programs
3. Review premium pricing structures
4. Increase customer engagement initiatives
5. Promote bundled telecom products
6. Identify and monitor high-risk customer segments

---

# Final Conclusion

The analysis identified customer age, pricing, and engagement levels as key drivers of churn. The XGBoost model achieved the highest predictive performance with 75.5% accuracy.

By implementing targeted retention strategies focused on high-risk customer segments, the organization can reduce churn, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term customer value.
